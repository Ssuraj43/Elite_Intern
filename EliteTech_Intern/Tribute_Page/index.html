Tribute to Sir Ratan Tata

About Ratan Tata

Sir Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He has been a pioneer in the business world, transforming the Tata Group into a global brand. Known for his humility and far-reaching contributions, he has left an indelible mark on society.

Key Achievements

  • Launched Tata Nano, the world's most affordable car.
  • Acquired global brands like Jaguar and Land Rover.
  • Expanded the Tata Group's reach to over 100 countries.
  • Contributed extensively to healthcare, education, and innovation.
"I don't believe in taking right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right." - Ratan Tata
